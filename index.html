About Fast Choice Homecare

A local, family-owned in-home care provider.

We know how hard it can be when someone you love begins to struggle with daily tasks. Fast Choice Homecare was created to give families a dependable, local option for support at home.

Our focus is on respect, reliability, and genuine compassion. Inviting a caregiver into your home is a big decision, and we do not take that trust lightly. We take the time to understand your routines, preferences, and goals before we ever step through the door.

From a few hours a week to more frequent visits, we design simple, clear care plans that fit your family’s schedule and budget. Our caregivers are selected for their patience, character, and commitment to treating clients like family.

  • Non-medical, in-home support tailored to each person.
  • Respectful, dignified assistance with everyday tasks.
  • Open communication with families and loved ones.
  • Flexible care options that can adjust as needs change.

Services

Everyday support to make life at home easier.

Every person and family is different. We start by listening, then build a care plan that fits your routine and priorities. All services are non-medical.

Companionship & Social Support

Conversation, games, walks, and activities that help reduce loneliness and keep your loved one engaged with daily life.

Assistance with Dressing & Personal Care

Discreet help with bathing, grooming, dressing, toileting, and safe mobility so your loved one can feel clean, comfortable, and confident.

Nutritional Care & Meal Support

Help with planning and preparing simple, healthy meals and snacks, grocery shopping support, and gentle encouragement around eating and hydration.

Light Housekeeping & Laundry

Tidying common areas, dishes, trash removal, sweeping, vacuuming, and laundry to help keep the home clean, safe, and welcoming.

Mobility Assistance & Support

Hands-on support with walking, transfers, and moving safely around the home or to and from appointments, helping reduce fall risk and increase confidence.

Respite for Family Caregivers

Scheduled breaks so family caregivers can rest, work, or handle personal responsibilities while knowing their loved one is being cared for.

Important: Fast Choice Homecare is a non-medical agency. We do not provide medication administration, injections, wound care, or other skilled medical services. We are happy to coordinate alongside your preferred healthcare providers.
